Namaste

The Indian cuisine served in the Taj is as diverse as its cultures, languages, regions and its climate. Every major region of India brings its own unique dishes and subtle variations to popular dishes. TAJ comes from Taj Mahal (Sometimes called Taj) is generally considered the finest example of Mughal architecture. The Taj Mahal is often described as one of the seven wonders of the modern world.

The dishes served and prepared at various TAJ at Myungdong, Bundang, Yeongtong are much loved as they are not only authentic but are served in a true Indian ambience. The Taj in the heart of seoul that is in Myungdong, Bundang, Yeongtong offers unique dining experience.
