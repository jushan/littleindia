Assalamulaikum

The Indian cuisine served in the Little India is as diverse as its cultures, languages, regions and its climate. Every major region of India brings its own unique dishes and subtle variations to popular dishes. Little India comes from Little India Mahal (Sometimes called Little India) is generally considered the finest example of Mughal architecture. The Little India Mahal is often described as one of the seven wonders of the modern world.

The dishes served and prepared at various Little India at Myungdong, Bundang, Yeongtong are much loved as they are not only authentic but are served in a true Indian ambience. The Little India in the heart of seoul that is in Myungdong, Bundang, Yeongtong offers unique dining experience.
